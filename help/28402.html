What if I don't accept a Feedite agreement?

Please remember that Feedite agreements and updates are only created to enforce guidelines that keep everyone's experience on the platform great. If you don't agree to an agreement or agreement update, however, please immediately discontinue use of Feedite. After discontinuation of Feedite usage, please email us at feediteapp@gmail.com stating you no longer wish to use the platform. We will try to delete your account information as soon as possible - but remember that if you use Feedite or Feedite services after this, you indicate agreement to the new service agreements.
